What Vektor does

Anomaly Detection

Vektor monitors every metric continuously and flags the ones that matter — with plain-language summaries, not just red dots on a chart.

Universal Connectors

Connect Snowflake, BigQuery, Postgres, Stripe, or any REST API in under two minutes. No ETL pipelines, no engineering ticket required.
